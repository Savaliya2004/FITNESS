Common Questions

How is this plan generated?
Your plan is generated dynamically from our meal database using the Mifflin-St Jeor TDEE formula and your fitness profile. Every meal is curated by the admin team — no hard-coded plans.
Can I regenerate the plan?
Your plan is managed by the administration team to ensure quality. If you need a new plan, please contact an admin or update your fitness profile goals and request a new assigned plan.
What if I see meals I can't eat?
Update your dietary preference in your profile, then regenerate. The system will only pull meals matching your preference (veg / eggetarian / non-veg).
